Website Bekasi Care

Bekasi Care is a platform for residents affected by flooding to report flood problems in their area. The website features a discussion system where community members can discuss flood-related issues and collaborate on solutions.

Users can also create articles about flood awareness and prevention. The platform includes a transparent reporting system that tracks progress from initial report until the flood issue is resolved, ensuring accountability throughout the process.
